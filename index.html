Our Menu

Americano

An americano is quite simply just hot water and espresso. It’ll either be served 1/2 and 1/2 or 1/3 espresso to 2/3 water.

Cappuccino

The ratio of milk to coffee in a cappuccino is 2:3 milk and 1:3 espresso. Therefore, a cappuccino is three equal parts steamed milk, milk foam, and espresso.

Caffe Latte

A latte is a coffee drink with espresso, steamed milk and a layer of foam on top.
